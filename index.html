How it's done

  1. Place batter boards and string according to stakes to assure the job is square
  2. Drill hole with auger at specific measurement and set piling leveled two feet short of desired depth
  3. Fill all space around piling with the extracted dirt, followed with tamping.
  4. Place pneumatic hammer on piling and count amount of blows per foot to reach compaction (kips). Each count is written on plans next to that piling
  5. Clean up and time for framers to follow in!
